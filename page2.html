More Productivity Tips

Tools and habits that actually work

Using the right tools can significantly improve productivity. Digital calendars, task managers, and note-taking apps help students stay organized and focused.

However, productivity is not just about tools. Healthy habits like proper sleep, exercise, and breaks are equally important.

Helpful Productivity Tools

  • Notion
  • Todoist

Daily Productivity Routine

  1. Plan tasks in the morning
  2. Complete most important task first
  3. Take breaks between study sessions
  4. Reflect on the day
